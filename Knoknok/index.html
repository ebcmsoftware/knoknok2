About Knoknok

Knoknok is a mini social network for you and your roommates, meant to make living together more convenient and seamless.

Make a Knoknok room and invite your roommates, and once you're all set up you can view the status of your room and set it. For instance, if you're going to sleep, set your Knoknok status to "asleep" to let your roommate know they should be quiet when they enter your room.
