Knoknok is the app for roommates!

What is it?

Knoknok helps answer the question "can I go into my room?" by providing roommmates with a private, simple cloud based message board where they can share the status of their room.

Why is that cool?

No more socks on doorknobs. Way less awkward situations.

Isn't this texting?

Discretely set a Knoknok status and check it on a need to know basis, before you enter your room. Don't unnecesarily interrupt your roommate with texts and stop filling your inbox with "you in the room?" texts.
